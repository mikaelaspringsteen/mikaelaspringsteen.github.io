What does it mean to study number in social life?

As a social technology, numbers are cultural objects, both in particular instances and as a class, and humans interact with numbers in ways both social and cultural, not just mathematical or technical. The rise of big data, an associated explosion of quantification, and a tendency toward commodification and financialization have rendered the cultural impact of numbers more salient than ever.
