The Day the Saucers Came

This cake takes as its inspiration another Neil Gaiman piece—"The Day the Saucers Came".{% marginnote "saucer-mn-id" "There is one small addition not drawn from the poem. I completed the cake shortly after the passing of the incomparable Sir Terry Pratchett. As another of my favorite authors, his works shaped much of who I am today and I included a small tribute to him on the top tier. I figured Neil Gaiman wouldn’t mind." %} It is meant to be 'read' from the top down, the same way in which you would read the original poem. The cake topper represents the title and first stanza. The top tier includes the name of the author—Neil Gaiman—positioned immediately beneath the 'title', just as it would be in a standard formatting of the printed poem. Each tier includes elements drawn from the corresponding stanza. The cake itself is a twist on the classic Southern hummingbird cake – which was modified to include white chocolate. The layers are filled with a cream cheese custard filling, and covered with a browned buttercream frosting. Each layer is then covered in chocolate.


Gingerbread House

A miniature gingerbread house, with an almond-shingle roof and red-hot doorknob, presented on a dried blood orange base.


Magyk

This cake mimicked the design of Magyk, the first book in Angie Sage’s Septimus Heap series.


A Calendar of Tales

This edible plate takes as inspiration Neil Gaiman’s A Calendar of Tales and, in particular, Jenn Bender’s submission{% marginfigure "jennbender-id" "assets/img/gallery/cake/jenn-bender-a-calendar-of-tales.jpg" "Jenn Bender's submission to the A Calendar of Tales Project"%} to the project. The plate itself is made of pastillage, with colors painted on with food coloring, and black piping gel used for contrast.


unBirthday Cake

This single-tier “unBirthday Cake” was inspired by Alice in Wonderland. Many thanks to NickyToons for granting permission to use her beautiful Alice in Wonderland Card Set. The cake is a rich, moist chocolate butter cake layered with fresh strawberry mousse and covered with a Swiss meringue buttercream.


Christmas

Created for the Edinburgh Estonian Society's 2018 Christmas party, this two-tiered hummingbird cake—filled and iced with a browned buttercream—was accompanied by sugar cookies, themselves filled with raspberry jam.
